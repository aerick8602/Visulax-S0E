The Sieve of Eratosthenes is an ancient algorithm for finding all prime numbers up to a specified integer.It efficiently identifies prime numbers without relying on factorization. Instead, it systematically eliminates non-prime numbers from a list of consecutive integers. For more detailed information and implementations check out GeeksforGeeks.
